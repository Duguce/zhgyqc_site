Exploring the **Temple of Heaven** (Tiantan) at night offers a completely different perspective from the daytime crowds. As dusk falls, the **Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests** is illuminated, its golden roof gleaming against the deepening blue of the twilight sky. It is a moment of perfect tranquility and majesty.
